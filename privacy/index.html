Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

At NimbleLabs, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our mobile app and website, why we collect it, and how we protect it. By using NimbleLabs, you agree to this policy.

1 Who We Are

NimbleLabs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the NimbleLabs mobile application available on iOS and Android, as well as the website located at nimblelabs.io. For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at privacy@nimblelabs.io.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information depending on how you use our app:

Category Examples How Collected
Account Information Name, email address, username, password (hashed), profile photo Provided by you at sign-up or via OAuth (Apple, Google)
Usage Analytics Features accessed, session duration, crash reports, device model, OS version, app version Collected automatically when you use the app
Location Data Approximate or precise GPS coordinates Collected only with your explicit permission via device settings
Technical Data IP address, device identifiers (IDFA/AAID), time zone, language settings Collected automatically

We do not collect payment information directly. Any in-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play, which have their own privacy policies.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate your identity
  • Provide, personalize, and improve the app's features and functionality
  • Analyze usage trends to identify bugs, performance issues, and areas for improvement
  • Use location data to deliver location-aware features within the app (only when permission is granted)
  • Send important service notifications, such as security alerts or policy updates
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data to serve third-party advertisements.

4 Legal Basis for Processing

Depending on your location, we rely on the following legal bases to process your personal information:

  • Contract performance — to provide you the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our app and prevent fraud
  • Consent — for location data and optional communications (you can withdraw consent at any time)
  • Legal obligation — where required by applicable law

5 Data Sharing

We share your data only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us operate our app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting). These providers are contractually bound to handle data securely and only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6 Location Data

Location access is optional. The app will request permission before accessing your device's location. You can grant or revoke this permission at any time through your device's settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Location Services; Android: Settings → Apps → NimbleLabs → Permissions).

If you deny location permission, all other features of the app will continue to function normally.

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes.

Aggregated, anonymized usage analytics may be retained indefinitely as they cannot be linked back to you.

8 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest, secure authentication practices, and access controls limiting who can view your data internally. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9 Children's Privacy

NimbleLabs is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us at privacy@nimblelabs.io and we will promptly delete it.

10 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — opt out of non-essential data collection or communications at any time
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw previously granted consent (e.g., location access)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@nimblelabs.io. We will respond within 30 days.

Our app or website may contain links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.
