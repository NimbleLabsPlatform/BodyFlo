4 How we use information

We use information to:

  • Create and maintain your account and sign you in.
  • Store and sync profile fields we host (e.g. name, fitness goal, sign-in provider) via Firebase / Firestore.
  • Store workout plans locally on your device (primary store for your plans in current versions).
  • Provide Smart plan and related features, including AI processing where applicable.
  • Operate subscriptions and free-tier limits (e.g. counting plan creations and completed workouts per calendar month in your local timezone).
  • Improve reliability, security, and product experience (including analytics and crash reporting where implemented).
  • Comply with law and enforce our Terms of Use.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to train third-party general-purpose AI models unless we clearly disclose a different practice in a future update.
